PaperFlow is a fully offline document scanner, PDF reader, and photo editor. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

PaperFlow may request Camera permission to scan documents and capture images. All captured content remains on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

PaperFlow does not use analytics, advertising, cloud storage, or tracking services.

6. Data Security

All information remains on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

7. Children's Privacy

PaperFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.
